Second Entry

Jason Lan, June 9, 2015. 11:00

Last hours of work

My computer started to have issues around the end of the work day. I tried to play with the VPN server so that I could better understand how it works and how I could create my own, but after I created a second VPN server, the first one stopped working, leaving me with no VPN connection. I couldn't find a way to fix the current VPN, which was located somewhere in Japan, so I searched the web for a new VPN. I managed to find a US VPN which worked so I was able to use email and google online. Afterwards, the computer internet stopped working altogether, except for QQ instant messaging. I couldn't use any web browsers so I just practiced the code I knew in Sublime and edited my website, since it is a local file and doesn't require internet connection. The end of the workday at 6 o'clock was nearing, so rather than spend more time fixing the internet I just reviewed what I had learned during my first workday.

After work

After work yesterday, I waited for MaoMao's husband to pick me up. He took me to a restuarant where I ate fish, boiling vegetables, and pork. The boiling vegetables was interesting because it was placed in a metal bowl over a container with solid alcohol which burned and boiled the vegetables as we ate. Afterwards, he took me to his house where I will be staying while I am in Nanjing. I settled in and placed my belongings to make myself feel at home. I watched a rerun of Game 2 of the NBA Finals on TV but I had to fast forward and just watch the end because I was falling asleep on the sofa. It was less exciting since I had already looked up the game result online. Still, the Cavaliers beating the Warriors was very unexpected. I went to sleep around 11 and woke up a couple times during the night because I still have some jet lagg.

Morning of Day 2

I got up around 7 in the morning to prepare to go to work at 9. I tried to make myself breakfast, but all I could find in the fridge were some eggs and yogurt. I searched the web for an easy way to fry eggs, and I tried my luck. I fried two eggs, but I could tell as soon as I poured the eggs into the pan that I added too much oil. My eggs were super puffy and mildly burnt, but I still ate them. Then I washed the dishes and drank some yogurt. I watched some TV as I waited for MaoMao's husband to wake up, but he didn't get up until about 8:40. He microwaved some buns for me and he drove me to my workplace at around 9:40.

Second Day of Work

Being late for work on my second day was slightly embarrassing, but my boss didn't seem to care too much. He told me I should work on learning the DOM (Document Object Model) for Javascript, which is able to change HTML and CSS elements. I spent the day reviewing CSS and learning Javascript, but I haven't added many new elements to my website yet. I also managed to install XAMPP which lets me host my website on a new server. For now, I'm not sure why that is important yet.
